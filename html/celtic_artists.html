Celtic woman.

Celtic Woman is an all irish group created by David Kavanagh, Sharon Browne and David Downes. They preform many genres such as celtic, folk music, new-age music and christian music. the members include Mairead carlin, Susan McFadden Eabha McMahon and Tara McNeill. for more information on the band go to their wiki page by clicking the button or go to their website at www.celticwoman.com
